Jiajia Jia's Biography

  • Since 2016, he is currently working towards the PhD degree in systems and control with the University of Groningen, Groningen, the Netherlands, supervised by Prof. Harry L. Trentelman and Prof. M. Kanat Camlibel.

  • In 2016, he received the Master degree in Control Science and Engineering from the China University of Mining and Technology, Xuzhou, P. R. China, supervised by Prof. Xiaoping Ma and Prof. Bin Ye .

  • In 2011, he received the Bachelor degree in Automation from the Jiangsu University of Science and Technology, Zhenjiang, P. R. China.

  • In 1989, Jiajia Jia was born in Xuzhou, P. R. China.
